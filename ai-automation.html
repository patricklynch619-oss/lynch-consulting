What Is AI Automation?

Most home service businesses lose 60% of their leads simply because they do not follow up fast enough or often enough. The average prospect needs 7 to 12 touchpoints before they book — and doing that manually for every lead is impossible.

We build a fully automated sales machine that responds to every lead within minutes, sends personalized follow-up sequences across email and SMS, scores leads based on intent, and books calls directly onto your calendar — all without you lifting a finger.

The result is a pipeline that runs 24 hours a day, 7 days a week, capturing and converting leads you would have otherwise lost.
