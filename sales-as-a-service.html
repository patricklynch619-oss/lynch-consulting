What Is Sales as a Service?

Hiring a full-time sales rep costs $60,000 to $90,000 per year — before benefits, training, and management time. And most sales hires for small home service businesses take 3 to 6 months to ramp up before they generate any real revenue.

Our Sales as a Service program gives you all the output of a dedicated sales team at a fraction of the cost.
